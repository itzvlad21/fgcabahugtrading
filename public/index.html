We also install Paint Protection Film, but what is Paint Protection Film (PPF?)

Ultimate Protection

For Your Vehicle

Paint Protection Film is a clear, thermoplastic urethane film that protects your vehicle's paint from scratches, chips, and stains. This virtually invisible shield maintains your car's value and keeps it looking newer, longer.

Self-Healing

Advanced technology allows the film to heal from minor scratches with heat exposure

UV Protection

Prevents paint oxidation and fading caused by harmful UV rays

Hydrophobic

Water-repelling properties make cleaning and maintenance easier

Long-Lasting

Durable protection that lasts for years with proper maintenance
